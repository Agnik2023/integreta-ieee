Integrata: Builders Week '26

Presented by IEEE Student Branch, Academy of Technology

Welcome to Integrata, AOT’s flagship technical symposium designed to bridge the gap between core algorithmic problem-solving and modern product development. Whether you are a competitive coder optimizing every line or a creative developer building user-centric apps, Integrata offers the ultimate arena to test your skills, learn from industry experts, and build portfolio-ready projects.

ROOTS at a glance

  • 15.05.2026 | Online quiz round from 6:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.
  • MCQ-based qualifying round on Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • 20.05.2026 | In-person DSA contest at ION Lab, from 2:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.
  • Separate sets for 1st Year and 2nd to 4th Year participants.

VIEW at a glance

  • 16.05.2026 to 18.05.2026 | Online prototyping phase from 9:00 p.m. (May 16) to 6:00 a.m. (May 18).
  • Teams receive real-world problem statements focused on UI/UX prototypes, wireframes, and basic architectural design.
  • Shortlisted teams advance to the offline development sprint for the final round.
  • 23.05.2026 | Finale at B Block Audi. from 10:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
  • Live pitching, professional sessions, final judging, and prize distribution for the App Dev track.
  • Team participation: 2 members per team.
